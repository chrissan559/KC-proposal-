The Agreement

Your Protection.
In Writing.

Every patron engagement is covered by a signed hire purchase loan agreement. This is a legal document — not a handshake. Below are the core terms.

Loan Structure
All patron capital is classified as a hire purchase loan — not equity. You do not own shares in KCLN. You are a creditor with a defined repayment schedule and a legal claim if terms are breached.
Repayment Terms
Fixed monthly repayments begin 30 days after funds are received. Repayment continues for 12 consecutive months. Total returned = principal + 10% flat return.
Grace & Penalty Clauses
Starter & Mid patrons: 14-day grace period. Missed payment rolls to next month. Growth & Anchor patrons: 7-day grace period. After grace, a ₦5,000 penalty applies per week of delay.
Equipment & Ownership
Patron capital funds specific business equipment. Equipment remains operational throughout the loan term. On full repayment, hire purchase completes and ownership transfers fully to KCLN.
Default Clause
In the event of default beyond the grace period, KCLN is liable for the outstanding balance as a personal debt obligation. The agreement is witnessed and legally binding under Nigerian contract law.
Transparency Rights
All patrons receive monthly revenue reports. Growth and Anchor patrons receive quarterly business reviews and full financial statements. Site visits are available on request at any time.
